Automatic Https

For automatic HTTPS for any website, we need two things: Trusted Certificate Authority that support automatic verification and provisioning of SSL certificate. A web-server/reverse-proxy that can automatically obtain the SSL certificate from above CA(s) and configure it. Let’s Encrypt is the perfect solution for the first requirement. Quoting from their official site: Let’s Encrypt is a free, automated, and open certificate authority (CA), run for the public’s benefit.
